FAQ

⚠ Does this violate Dice.com's Terms of Service?

Yes. Automated job applications are against Dice's ToS. The bot includes anti-detection measures (Bezier mouse paths, human typing delays, fingerprint spoofing) but no tool guarantees zero account risk. Use it at your own discretion.

Which jobs does it apply to?

Easy Apply jobs only — the ones with a one-click apply button hosted on Dice. Jobs that redirect to external ATS platforms (Workday, Greenhouse, Lever) are skipped automatically.

Does it customize my resume per application?

No. The same resume PDF is uploaded to every application. You point RESUME_PATH in .env to a single file. Resume rotation per keyword is on the roadmap.

How many applications per day is safe?

250–300/day is the recommended rate. Running above 300/day increases the risk of account action. Control throughput via BATCH_SIZE and BATCH_BREAK_MINUTES in config.py.
