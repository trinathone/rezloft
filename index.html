★ FAQ

Common questions

Will Dice ban my account? ▾
Bot runs at 250–300/day with 20 layers of anti-detection — Bezier mouse curves, human typing simulation, canvas fingerprint noise, WebGL spoofing, and rotating viewports. Running since April 2026 with zero blocks across 8,202 applications.
Is my Dice password safe? ▾
100% yes. Everything runs locally on YOUR machine. No servers. No data collection. No cloud. Code is fully open source — verify it yourself line by line on GitHub. Your credentials never leave your computer.
Does it work on Windows and Mac? ▾
Yes. Python + Playwright runs on both. The setup wizard guides you through every step with zero technical knowledge needed — it takes under 3 minutes. Full separate instructions for Windows and Mac in START_HERE.md.
What keywords should I use? ▾
Ask ChatGPT or Claude: "Give me 30 Dice.com C2C job search keywords for a [your role] engineer with skills in [your skills]. One per line, no bullets." Then paste into keywords.txt. The setup wizard prompts you with this exact question.
